Programming S.I.G.

cat about.txt

The Programming S.I.G. is an open community for Franklin students of every background to explore programming together. We welcome absolute beginners and experienced developers alike, focusing on learning by doing. We enhance our programming skills through collaborative projects, peer mentoring, and engaging workshops. Our members build tools, experiment with new technologies, and connect classroom knowledge to real-world applications, all while supporting each other's growth. Whether your goal is career preparation, research, or personal creativity, this is a place to learn, contribute, and belong.

cat mission.txt

  1. Our mission is to create a supportive, accelerated environment where Franklin students can explore programming in practical, meaningful ways.
  2. We emphasize hands-on collaboration, peer learning, and project-based discovery-helping members develop technical confidence, professional skills, and creative problem-solving abilities.
  3. By bridging coursework with real-world challenges, the Programming SIG prepares students to innovate, contribute, and lead in technology-driven fields.
